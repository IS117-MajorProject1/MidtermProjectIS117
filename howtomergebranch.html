    How To Merge a Branch in Webstorm


    In this section I will show you how to take the new branch you just made and merge it into your master branch. You can merge on github or in webstorm. First we should define some terms to help you understand.


    Merge: Merging will take the changes from your current branch and put them in your master branch. You will do this when you like the content in your current branch and you know the code works.


    Checkout: Checkout lets you view the branch. When you click to view all your braches, you can then select a branch you're not currently in and click checkout. This will show you everything in that branch. It is useful to see changes you made between branches.


    As you see in the picture above im in the checkoutDefine branch. Now I like what I put in this branch and I want to merge it into the master but how do we do that. You should go to where all your branches are in the bottom right of webstorm, click your master branch and select checkout. Next go back to your branches and select the branch you want to merge into your master and select, "Merge into Current". You have just merge that branch into your master branch.

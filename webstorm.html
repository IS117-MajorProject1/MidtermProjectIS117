    Webstorm and AFS

    My name is Andy Marmolejos. I will be showing the following tutorials for Webstorm and AFS. The tutorials will consist of setting up Webstorm. How to setup deployment to deploy the site. Making a new project using HTML5 Boilerplate with Grunt. Webstorm is a great IDE program for building websites due to their large range of features, such as Git, AFS and more.


    For AFS, I will show the process on how to use the terminal. How to create, copy files, etc. How to setup AFS for the website and access the AFS through it. How to deploy your work to AFS and be able to see your results in the website.
